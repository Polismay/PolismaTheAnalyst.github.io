Me

An avid animal lover, bookworm, travel enthusiast, and foodie, I find joy in the simple pleasures of life.

Hey there! I'm Polisma, a 25-year-old from Nepal who took a leap to the USA at 19 to chase my dreams. These days, I'm navigating the vibrant chaos of NYC, working at an accounting firm, and diving into the challenges of a master's degree.

But outside the hustle, my heart belongs to furry friends – animals bring me pure joy. Weekends turn into mini-adventures exploring cozy bookstores, cute cafes, and quiet parks. Interestingly, my move to New York sparked a newfound love for reading. Now, I'm an avid reader, always searching for a good book to keep me company on subway rides.

In those precious free moments, you'll find me in my kitchen cooking up a storm or on my little balcony, tending to my growing garden. Food is my passion, and experimenting with flavors is my thing. Gardening, to me, is like therapy – there's something magical about watching those plants thrive. Currently, life for me is all about trying to strike that balance between work, study, and life's simple joys.
